About Me

关于我

I work at the intersection of industrial design, engineering and global retail execution. I turn brand visions into physical retail spaces across 20+ countries — every project ships on time, every detail matters.

My current role focuses on retail display projects for international brands — coordinating design development, technical details, supplier communication, production follow-up and export delivery. Global brand projects include Nespresso, IWC, HUGO BOSS and others.

我拥有工业设计背景，目前在德企担任技术工程师，主要负责全球品牌零售展示项目的工程深化、生产协调与出口交付。
我的优势在于同时理解设计语言、制造工艺与跨境项目管理，能够将品牌概念转化为可落地的终端展示方案。
